Common questions
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. Your 30-day free trial starts immediately with no credit card required. You only need to add payment details if you decide to continue after the trial.
What happens after my free trial?
After 30 days you'll be asked to choose a plan. If you don't upgrade, you keep access to basic features only. No surprise charges — ever.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade any time. If you upgrade mid-month you're only charged the difference. If you downgrade it takes effect at the next billing date.
How does annual billing work?
Pay upfront for 12 months and get 2 months completely free — that's like paying for 10 and getting 12. Annual plans can be cancelled but are non-refundable after 30 days.
What's the difference between plans?
Single Coach is for one coach managing one team. Multi Coach adds up to 5 coaches with shared libraries. Full Club gives unlimited coaches with club branding and analytics.
Is DrillGen right for junior coaches?
100%. DrillGen was built specifically for grassroots coaches in Australia. Every drill and session plan is age-appropriate from U6 through to senior level.
